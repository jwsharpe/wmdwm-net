Practice Drills

These drills are in priority order. Work through them in order. Take the top most drill you are still uncomfortable with and drill them for 5 minutes (up to 20 if you can stay focused). When a drill feels consistent, move to the next one.

  1. Counter-strafing — until consistent
  2. Mouse swiping — until boring to practice
  3. Fast aim / reflex (standing)
  4. Fast aim / reflex (strafing)
1. Counter-strafing drill

Practices: counter strafing, micro adjusting, effective weapons firing

Setup: Aim map (e.g. csstats aim). Bots along a wall. AK or M4, rifle out.

Each rep:

  1. Hold D, strafe along the wall.
  2. When a bot's head is visible, tap A to counter-strafe and stop.
  3. Micro-adjust to the head.
  4. Fire 3–5 bullets.
  5. Keep strafing to the wall, then run the same pattern going left.
2. Mouse swiping drill

Practices: mouse swiping, flicking, micro adjusting, effective weapons firing

Setup: Aim map with bots spread so you must turn between targets. Stand still.

Each rep:

  1. Mouse at pad center.
  2. Take a large swipe.
  3. Lift mouse, recenter on pad.
  4. Flick, then micro-adjust to head.
  5. Fire 3–6 bullets
  6. Recenter. Next bot.
3. Fast aim / reflex — standing drill

Practices: tracking, mouse swiping, micro adjusting, effective weapons firing

Setup: Aim map with stationary bots. Stand still.

Each rep:

  1. Recenter mouse on pad.
  2. Swipe until you spot a bot.
  3. Track the bot's head for 1–2 seconds.
  4. Fire 3–6 bullets while tracking, crosshair on head.
  5. Recenter. Next bot.
4. Fast aim / reflex — strafing drill

Practices: tracking, mouse swiping, counter strafing, flicking, micro adjusting, effective weapons firing

Each rep:

  1. Strafe with A or D.
  2. Swipe until you spot a bot.
  3. Tap the opposite movement key to counter-strafe and stop.
  4. Flick to head, then micro-adjust to head.
  5. Track head ~0.5–1 s (longer than in a match on purpose).
  6. Fire 3–6 bullets while finishing the track.
  7. Repeat; alternate strafe direction when you can.
